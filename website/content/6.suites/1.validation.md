## What the table shows

Below is every case in the **validation** test suite, rendered straight from the suite data — section, case, what it checks, and a ⚠ when there's something subtle worth reading. Nothing here is hand-written prose about the validator; it's the suite itself.

Hover (or tap / focus) any row and it expands to reveal the **type declaration** it was generated from, the equivalent `RT.*` **schema** when one exists, and the **actual compiled JavaScript** the build emits for it. The generated function is the real thing your bundle ships — no reflection, no schema object, just a specialized check.
